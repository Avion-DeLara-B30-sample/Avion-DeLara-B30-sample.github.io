Uncle Roger, whose real name is Nigel Ng, is a Malaysian stand-up comedian and YouTuber. He gained popularity for his comedic reaction videos to cooking videos, particularly ones that feature egg-fried rice. Uncle Roger's humorous and exaggerated commentary, often in a faux Cantonese accent, has captivated a global audience.
